Grilled Salmon!

Salmon is a heart healthy fish that has many omegas and other nutrient rich properties!

How do I make such a delicious treat?

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lb. of Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Pesto
  • Butter
  • 2-3 Lemons

Steps to cook

  1. Preheat a hot skillet to high --This is where those flat tops shine!
  2. Rub Salmon with butter, giving it even coat
  3. Add a liberal amount of Salt, Pepper, and Paprika to taste
  4. Lightly coat the salmon with fresh pesto
  5. Thinly slice one Lemon
  6. Once the flat top or skillet is hot enough, add butter then immediately add the salmon flesh side down when the butter is melted
  7. Flip after 4-5 minutes, the fish should have a nice char, and add the thinly sliced lemons on top
  8. Cook for an additional 4-5 minutes, serve hot with additional slices of lemons to squeeze on top!
