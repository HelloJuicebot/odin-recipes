The mighty, undisputed champion of sandwiches

The PB&J

This sandwich rules above all others. Hungry in the middle of the night? Perfect. Breakfast? Perfect.

Not only is it delicious as all hell, but it will also make your squats hit depth.

What will you need?

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly
  • Optional: a big glass of milk

How do I make such a creation? Am I god?

  1. Get your two slices of bread (Toasted? Up to you)
  2. Slather on 2 or 8 tablespoons of peanutbutter
  3. Give it some of that sweet, sweet, jelly
  4. Stick both pieces together
  5. Cut the sandwich in half, or in 4ths if you want more sandwich
